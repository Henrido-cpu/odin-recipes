Recipe for a Chicken Caeasar Pasta.

Delicious and easy to follow recipe:

Ingredients and steps to follow

  1. 1 pound dry penne pasta
  2. 1 tablespoon butter
  3. 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves-cut into 1 inch cubes
  4. 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  5. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  6. 1 (8 ounce) bottle Caesar salad dressing
  7. 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  8. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  9. 1 head romain lettuce - rinsed, dried and shredded
  10. 1 large tomato chopped
