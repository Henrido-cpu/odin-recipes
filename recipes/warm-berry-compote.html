Recipe for a Warm Berry Compote

Delicious and easy to follow recipe:

Ingredients and steps to follow

  1. 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  2. 1/2 cup white sugar
  3. 1/4 cup orange juice
  4. 1 1/2 teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  5. 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  6. 2 tablespoons water
