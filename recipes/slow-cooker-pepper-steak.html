Slow Cooker Pepper Steak

Recipe for a Slow Cooker Pepper Steak

Delicious and easy to follow recipe:

Ingredients and steps to follow

  1. 2 pounds beef sirloin, cut into 2 inch strips
  2. 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
  3. 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  4. 1 cube beef bouillon
  5. 1/4 cup hot water
  6. 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  7. 1/2 cup chopped onion
  8. 2 large green bell peppers, roughly chopped
  9. 1(14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
  10. 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  11. 1 teaspoon white sugar
  12. 1 teaspoon salt
